Accounting and Finance Equations

Overall Value

Please Enter Two Values

Total Assets(TA) = Liabilities(L) + Equity(E)


Net Income

Please Enter Two Values

Net Income(NI) = Revenue(R) - Expenses(E)


Cash Ratio

Please Enter Two Values

Cash Ratio(CR) = Cash(C) / Current Liabilities(CL)


Profit Margin

Please Enter Two Values

Profit Margin(PM) = Net Income(NI) / Sales(S)


Debt/Equity Ratio

Please Enter Two Values

Debt/Equity Ratio(DER) = Total Liabilities(TL) /
Total Equity(TE)


Cost of Goods Sold

Please Enter Three Values

Cost of Goods Sold(CGS) =
Beginning Inventory(BI) +
Cost of Purchasing New Inventory(CPNI) -
Ending Inventory(EI)


Retained Earnings

Please Enter Three Values

Retained Earning(RE) =
Beginning Retained Earnings(BRE) +
(Net Income or Net Loss)(NINL) -
Cash Dividends(CD)
